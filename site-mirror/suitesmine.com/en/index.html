Luxury Apartments with Amenities

Suites Mine is a complex of 39 hotel apartments located just two blocks from Ángel de la Independencia, in Colonia Cuauhtémoc, CDMX.

We offer an exceptional lodging experience  for travelers looking for a high-quality property with a great location.

Our guests enjoy spacious rooms equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, as well as various additional amenities, including a bar, jacuzzi, terrace and an a la carte menu.

To ensure a quiet and comfortable environment, children under 6 years of age are not accepted, nor are parties or gatherings allowed in the rooms. We ask all our guests to respect the rules of coexistence to maintain a pleasant atmosphere for everyone.

Artes y Cultura
Exhibitions and Museum

The Museum Soumaya is an iconic art museum located in Mexico City, founded by the Carlos Slim Foundation. Its main building, with a futuristic design covered in hexagonal panels, is located in the Polanco area and is one of the most recognized architectural attractions in the city.

This museum houses an extensive art collection that includes pieces from pre-Hispanic times to the 20th century, highlighting works by European artists such as Rodin, Dalí and Monet, as well as Mexican artists. Admission is free, with the aim of democratizing access to art
